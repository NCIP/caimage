What's New?
April 20, 2006
 Anatomy and Disease Vocabularies updated
 caIMAGE utilizes now anatomy and disease vocabularies served by the NCI Thesaurus, a knowledgebase containing the working vocabularies for clinical, translational and basic research used in NCI data systems. The vocabularies for mouse diseases are a consensus product of the MMHCC Pathology Working Group.
August 6, 2004
 caIMAGE supports new viewer
 caIMAGE now supports the Zoomify viewer which allows fast interactive zooming and panning. The advanced version of the viewer is capable of displaying annotations, labels, and notes for an image.

July 1, 2004
 LPGimage Database of Lung Tumor TMAs
 The July 1, 2004 issue of Clinical Cancer Research includes the paper "Chromatin Remodeling Factors and BRM/BRG1 Expression as Prognostic Indicators in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer". The image database of lung TMAs can be accessed at LPGimage.

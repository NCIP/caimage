Submission of multiple images

For submission of multiple images we recommend the following procedure:
  1. Download the image_submission.zip file
  2. Extract the following files from the zip file:
    - image_annotations.xls
    - How to retrieve EVS numbers.ppt
    - instructions.doc
    - vocabulary_mouse_strains.xls
    - vocabulary_staining.xls
    - vocabulary_mouse_organs_tissues.xls
  3. Read the file "instruction.doc"
  4. Enter the information about your images in the "image_annotations.xls" Excel spreadsheet
  5. Burn your images and the Excel spreadsheet on a CD
  6. Mail the CD to

    NCI Center for Bioinformatics
    Att. caImage Team
    6116 Executive Blvd.
    Suite 403 MSC 8335
    Rockville, MD 20852-8335

  7. Please provide us with your email adress and telephone number in case we need to contact you.
If you need assistance, please contact Dr. Fei Xu via email xuf@mail.nih.gov
